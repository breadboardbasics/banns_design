Welcome to Banns Design

We are a concierge wedding florist based out of Atlanta, Georgia.

Our name: Banns Design

Banns (of marriage): “proclamation or notice given, especially in church, of a proposed marriage”

After much soul searching for a name fitting for luxury wedding florals, our founder came across an antiquated word, “banns” whilst reading about wedding history. Banns perfectly sums up the wedding season, meaning a proclamation or notice given of a proposed marriage. As Banns Design focuses on exclusively wedding flowers, as well as the planning that goes into the time leading up to weddings, this name seemed perfectly fitting.

Investment

While Banns Design offers concierge services throughout the wedding design and floral planning process, our additional services vary client to client. All the communication and planning that accompanies the wedding season is at the heart of the floral product from Banns Design, and thus is included in the base investment. For us, the design process is not a one-time planning, but a curation of your vision for your big day. When you plan your floral design with us, you receive not only an end product that any skilled florist could produce, but an experience of service and florals of your dreams.

Base services include but are not limited to, email, text and phone communication throughout your engagement, design and floral meetings or planning, any communication necessary to solidify your vision, and communication with your wedding contacts to ensure your bridal florals flawlessly compliment your venue, dress, wedding party, vision and most of all you on your big day. Additional services we proudly offer include on site, day of floral refreshing, design previews and mock ups, charitable repurposing of flowers, live event floral design for guests as favors or entertainment, 24/7 on-call design help, and more floral experience planning.
