Welcome to Banns Design

We are a concierge wedding florist based out of Atlanta, Georgia.

Our name: Banns Design

Banns (of marriage): “proclamation or notice given, especially in church, of a proposed marriage”

After much soul searching for a name fitting for luxury wedding florals, our founder came across an antiquated word, “banns” whilst reading about wedding history. Banns perfectly sums up the wedding season, meaning a proclamation or notice given of a proposed marriage. As Banns Design focuses on exclusively wedding flowers, as well as the planning that goes into the time leading up to weddings, this name seemed perfectly fitting.

Our Services

We offer clients concierge services throughout the wedding design and floral planning process. Communication and planning that accompanies the wedding season is at the heart of the floral product from Banns Design. The design process is not a one-time planning, but a curation of your vision for your big day. From start to finish, we are at your beck and call to execute the design you envision for your big day. Thus, when you plan your floral design with us, you receive not only an end product that any skilled florist could produce, but an experience of service and florals of your dreams.

Services include but are not limited to, email, text and phone communication throughout your engagement, design and floral mockups and meetings, any communication necessary to solidify your vision, and communication with your wedding contacts to ensure your bridal florals flawlessly compliment your venue, dress, wedding party, vision and most of all you on your big day.
